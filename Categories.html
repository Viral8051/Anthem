Diversity, Equity, & Inclusion

Honors for organizations and or work that supports, protects, represents and uplifts different groups of people or denounces racism, intolerance, and exclusion. Work may be focused on race, ethnicity, gender equality, sexual orientation and gender identity and rights, indigenous groups, socioeconomic status, language, culture, national origin, age, different abilities and religious commitments and political perspective.

Human & Civil Rights

Honors for organizations and or work that protect, defend and advance the rights of citizens to political and social freedom and equality regardless of race, religion, or other characteristics. Examples include programs that consider; democracy, civic engagement, freedom of the press, religion and equal justice under the law.

Humanitarian Action & Services

Honors for organizations and or work that prevent and relieve human suffering, including programs that; provide relief and support in crisis (environmental, peace and conflict resolution and fragile states, refugee and migrant protection). As well as projects that aid in economic development and fight against poverty, hunger, human trafficking and homelessness.

Sustainability, Environment, & Climate

Honors for organizations and work that focus on sustainability, climate and environmental issues including concerns for environmental conservation and the improvement of the state of the environment and all living things. Specific examples include projects that consider the impact of the climate emergency on the planet and its species, oceans, lakes, rivers and streams, nature conservancy, animal welfare, recycling and sustainable living.

Education, Art, & Culture

Honors for organizations and or work focused on; making learning possible for all students, the promotion of artistic and cultural excellence and the perseverance of artistic and cultural heritage. Education programs may help make learning more effective and more accessible to students of all backgrounds including; Adult education, early childhood programs, policy reform, financial support, special education and youth services.

Art and Cultural programs may include the promotion of art and artists from the stage, to the screen, to exhibition halls and public spaces. Projects may also focus on art for change, healing and connecting communities. Cultural programs could include the preservation of public spaces and landmarks, festivals, celebrations of ethnic experiences.

Health

Honors for organizations and or work that help to cure diseases, treat and support sick and disabled people, seek improvements in medical treatments, and promote public understanding and awareness of particular health risks, diseases, different abilities and staying fit and improving health. Projects could include; research, raising awareness and funding for disease and health related causes, patient and family support, innovation in health technologies and solutions, and organizations or programs focused on physical and mental health and wellbeing including sport, fitness, meditation.

Responsible Technology

Honors for organizations and or work focused on ensuring that digital technology has a positive impact on society, and that it is affordable, accessible, equitable and secure. Projects may focus on responsible technology, public programs and policy solutions, skills development, protecting freedom of expression, data collection and analysis, open source libraries and tools, games, among other things.
