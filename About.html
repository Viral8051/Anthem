About us

Over the years, we’ve seen people across the globe change their habits, moving us all towards a more purpose-driven life. Over the years, we’ve seen people across the globe change their habits, moving us all towards a more purpose-driven life. More than ever, we’re consciously thinking about the personal and global impact of the food we eat, the media we consume, the clothing we wear, the causes we support, and how we choose to spend our money.

Presented by The International Academy of Digital Arts and Sciences, The Anthem Awards recognize the breadth of mission-driven work (online and offline) around the globe by honoring the organizations, brands, and people creating real impact to make the world a better place. We hope that by celebrating and championing this work, we can amplify the voices making a significant impact and do our part to spark global change.

Whether you’re a brand, a not-for-profit, an agency, a foundation, a small team, or anything in-between, you can add your voice to The Anthem and define a new benchmark for purposeful work that ignites the next generation to shape the future.

Honoring the people, work, and voices that unite us, as one, for a better future.
